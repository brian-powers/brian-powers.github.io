01 — About
Background

I'm a network and systems analyst with deep experience managing enterprise infrastructure for municipal government. My day-to-day involves Juniper switches and routers, Palo Alto firewalls, Nutanix hyperconverged clusters, and VMware/ESXi environments.

Before transitioning into civilian IT, I served 22 years as an Active Guard Reserve Soldier in the Alabama Army National Guard, retiring as Sergeant Major. My final technical role as S6 NCOIC gave me hands-on responsibility for signal and IT support operations across multiple units.

I hold a B.S. in Computer Information Science with a Cybersecurity concentration from ECPI University and am working toward an MA in Executive Leadership. Long-term, I'm interested in IT infrastructure leadership, consulting, and teaching.
